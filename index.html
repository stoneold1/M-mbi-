The Cosmological Arc
Mwenenyaga
The Absolute. Source as Light. The unmanifest ground of all being. Pure presence before form.

Living Terms
Kĩrĩnyaga
Mount Kenya. Axis of the world. The mountain where heaven and earth converse. Home of Ngai.
Ituĩka
The great cycle of generational renewal. The transfer of power, land, and sacred authority between generations.
Thahu
Spiritual defilement or imbalance. A state of broken sacred relationship requiring restoration.
Kũhũranĩra
The act of restoration. Returning to right relationship with the ancestors, the land, and one another.
Kiama
Council of elders. Living governance rooted in sacred authority and accumulated wisdom.
Rũracio
The covenant bridge between families. Sacred exchange that binds two lineages in right relationship.
Mwethĩ
Communal sacred labor. The work done together — not for payment, but for the life of the whole.
Mũgũmo
The wild fig tree. The place of prayer and direct communion with Ngai. The tree that stands at the threshold.
Mũkũyũ
The domesticated fig. The place of covenant, ceremony, and community witness.
Gĩthĩmo
Sacred prohibition. The boundary between the permissible and the forbidden — the fence of the sacred.
Kũrathima
To bless or curse through the spoken word. The power of the mouth as creative and destructive force.
Ngoro
The heart-soul seat. The inner place where spirit and body meet. The center of felt knowing.
